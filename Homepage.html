Milwaukee is a neighbor to the great Lake Michigan which is filled with some of the worlds largest fresh water fish. Lake Michigan can provide a great fishing experience weather it be on shore or fishing from a boat. Some of the most common large fish which can be found here include Salmon, Trout and Northern Pikes. Each kind of fish is attracted to a different type of lure, which will be explained in detail on the links provided.
